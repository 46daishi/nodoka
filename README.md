## ✨ Features

- ⏱️ **Pomodoro mode** — stay on track with structured focus and break intervals
- 🌊 **Flow mode** — open-ended timer for deep work, with breaks sized as a percentage of your session
- 👤 Switch between profiles for different kinds of work or routines
- ✏️ Name your sessions to keep your day organized — autocompletes from your session history
- 🎨 14 themes (8 dark, 6 light)
- 📈 Track your progress over time with simple, readable charts and a session name breakdown
- 🎵 Ambient sounds and white noise to help you stay in the zone
- 🎮 Let others know you're focusing via detailed Discord Rich Presence
